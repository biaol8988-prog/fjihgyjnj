# BX-001 — Void Candle Dragon（虚烛龙）

## Classification
- ID: BX-001
- Type: Primordial Myth Beast
- Threat Level: L5 (Reality-Class Anomaly)
- Origin: Unknown / Pre-Creation Layer

---

## Habitat
Observed only in "non-geometric zones" of reality:
- Regions where time flow is inconsistent
- Dream-state overlaps
- Deep ocean trenches with no mapped coordinates

It is never recorded in a fixed location twice.

---

## Description
The Void Candle Dragon is described as a massive serpentine entity composed of dim, flickering candlelight wrapped around an absence of matter.

Its body is not illuminated by light—it emits the absence of darkness.

Observers report that looking directly at it causes:
- Memory fragmentation
- Loss of temporal orientation
- False recollection of events that never occurred

---

## Abilities

### 1. Temporal Snuffing
It can extinguish localized time flow, causing events to "skip" without continuity.

### 2. Memory Rewriting Drift
Nearby observers gradually rewrite their own memories to include encounters that never happened.

### 3. Candle Flame Reversal
Any source of fire or energy near it burns backward into fuel.

---

## Behavior
- Non-aggressive unless observed directly
- Appears to avoid documentation systems
- Responds unpredictably to measurement attempts

---

## Historical Record
First referenced in fragmented archival text:
> "When the seventh light was extinguished, the serpent of un-time awoke beneath the sea that does not exist."

---

## Containment Status
Impossible.
All known containment attempts result in:
- Observer disappearance
- Data corruption
- Self-deletion of records

---

## Notes
Some researchers hypothesize it is not a creature, but a system-level error in reality's construction given form.

---

Status: Active anomaly
